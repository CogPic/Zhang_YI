About

Yi Zhang is an Associate Chief Physician at Changzhou Second People's Hospital. Zhang has extensive clinical experience in neurology and is engaged in both medical practice and professional service in the fields of neuropsychology, psychosomatic medicine, and neurological quality control.

Professional Roles

  • Member, Neuropsychology Group, Neurology Branch, Jiangsu Medical Association
  • Committee Member, Psychosomatic Medicine Branch, Changzhou Medical Association
  • Committee Member, Medical Psychology Committee, Changzhou Psychological Society
  • Secretary, Quality Control Center of Neurology, Changzhou

Clinical Expertise

Specializes in neurological disorders including cerebrovascular disease, insomnia, anxiety, depression, cognitive and memory decline, dizziness, headache, Parkinson's disease, and peripheral neuropathy.
